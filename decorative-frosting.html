Cake Decorating Frosting

Ingredients (Single Batch)

  • ½ cup Shortening or Crisco
  • 1 lb (3 ¾ cup) Confectioner’s Sugar (powdered sugar)
  • 1 ½ teaspoon cornstarch
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt (to cut sweetness)
  • ¼ cup hot water
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla

Ingredients (Double Batch)

  • 1 cup Shortening or Crisco
  • 2 lb (7 ½ cup) Confectioner’s Sugar (powdered sugar)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (to cut sweetness)
  • ½ cup hot water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients into the bowl of your large standard mixer.
  2. Use the wire whip attachment and mix at a slow speed until well blended.
  3. Move up to high speed and beat for at least 2 minutes, stopping once or twice to scrape the sides of the bowl. Whip for one more minute.
  4. Make sure you keep the bowl of frosting covered with a damp paper towel when decorating the cake so it doesn’t dry out.
