Pizza

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal (to prevent sticking)
  • Tomato sauce
  • ½ cup of mozzarella cheese or another of your choice

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425˚.
  2. Sprinkle pizza pan with cornmeal to prevent dough from sticking.
  3. Push the air out of the dough and cut into 4 equal pieces. Roll out dough and transfer to the pan.
  4. Build up the edge slightly.
  5. Spread sauce on dough and add ½ cup of cheese and toppings of your choice.
  6. Bake pizza for 15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
