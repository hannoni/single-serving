Chocolate-Sour Cream Cake

Time
Prep Time:
45 mins
Bake Time:
25 mins
Total Time:
70 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup shortening
  • ½ cup + 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • ⅕ oz. bittersweet chocolate, melted and cooled
  • 4 oz. sour cream (½ cup)
  • ½ cup milk

Directions

  1. Let eggs stay at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the oven 350˚.
  3. Grease and flour one 9-inch round cake pan. Set aside.
  4. In a small bowl combine flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. In a large bowl beat shortening and sugar on medium speed until combined.
  6. Beat in eggs and vanilla until combined.
  7. Beat in melted chocolate and sour cream.
  8. Alternately add flour mixture and milk; beat on low after each addition just until combined.
  9. Spread batter in prepared pan.
  10. Bake for 25 minutes or until the tops spring back when lightly touched or a toothpick comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Remove from pan, cool.
