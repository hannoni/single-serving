Waffles

Makes 4-6 section waffles

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 cups flour

Directions

  1. Lightly brush grids with oil, close iron and heat.
  2. In a small bowl, sift together sugar, baking powder, salt, and flour. These are the dry ingredients.
  3. In a small bowl, beat egg whites until they are stiff peaks and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, beat egg yolks until thick and lemon colored.
  5. Continue beating yolks while adding milk and oil.
  6. Add dry ingredients, beat until smooth and fold in egg whites.
  7. Pour 1 cup of batter on a heated grid, close the unit and bake.
