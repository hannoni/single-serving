Blueberry Lemon Muffins

12 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup melted margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 8 ounces plain whole milk yogurt
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon lemon rind
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine eggs, margarine, and sugar in a large mixing bowl; beat until smooth. Stir in yogurt.
  3. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and lemon rind; mix just until moistened.
  4. Fold in blueberries.
  5. Spoon into greased (or paper lined) muffin cups.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes.
