Chocolate Swirl Coffee Cake

9 servings

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup flaked coconut
  • ¼ cup chopped nuts
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter or margarine melted
  • 2 cups buttermilk baking mix (Bisquick)
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ⅓ cup semisweet chocolate pieces, melted

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 400˚.
  2. Grease 8x8x2 inch square pan.
  3. Stir together coconut, nuts, ¼ cup sugar, and 1 tablespoon of the melted butter; set.
  4. Mix baking mix, ¼ cup sugar, remaining melted butter, the egg, and milk.
  5. Beat vigorously for 30 seconds. Pour into the pan.
  6. Spoon chocolate over batter; with knife, cut through batter several times for a marbled effect.
  7. Sprinkle coconut mixture evenly over top. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Serve warm.
