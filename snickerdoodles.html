Snicker Doodles

Makes about 36 cookies

Ingredients

  • ½ cup margarine, room temperature
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Cinnamon-Sugar Mixture:

  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375˚.
  2. In a medium bowl, beat margarine and sugar for 5 to 8 minutes until light and fluffy and creamy.
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla until blended.
  4. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt over the sugar mixture. Stir with a wooden spoon to make a stiff dough.
  5. Shape dough into walnut-sized balls; roll in cinnamon sugar mixture to coat.
  6. Place balls about 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until lightly brown. Remove from baking sheets; cool on wire racks.
