Nutty Sticky Rolls

12 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup light corn syrup or maple syrup
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • ⅓ cup coarsely chopped pecans or walnuts
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅓ cup shortening
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425˚.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine syrup, brown sugar, butter, water.
  3. Cook and stir over low heat until butter melts; DO NOT BOIL.
  4. Spread onto the bottom of a 9x9x2 inch baking pan.
  5. Sprinkle nuts over syrup mixture.
  6. In a mixing bowl stir together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  7. Cut in ⅓ cup shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  8. Make a well in the center.
  9. Add milk all at once, stirring just till dough clings together.
  10. On a lightly floured surface knead dough gently for 15-20 strokes.
  11. Roll dough into a 12x10 inch rectangle.
  12. Combine sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle over dough.
  13. Roll up from one of the long sides.
  14. Slice into 1 inch pieces.
  15. Place the cut side down onto nuts in the baking pan.
  16. Bake for about 25 minutes or until golden.
  17. Immediately loosen sides and invert onto a serving plate.
  18. Spoon any toppings in the pan over rolls.
  19. Serve warm.
