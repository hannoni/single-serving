Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ to 3 ¾ cups all purpose flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 rounded tablespoon active dry yeast (or 1 package)
  • ½ cup very warm water (120˚-130˚)
  • ½ cup very warm milk (120˚-130˚)
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Mix 2 cups of the flour, the sugar, oil, salt, and yeast using the electric mixer and white flat beater.
  2. Add water, milk, and egg. Beat on low speed, scraping bowl frequently, 1 minute. Stir in enough remaining flour to make dough easy to handle.
  3. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface: knead until smooth and elastic, about 5 minutes.
  4. Place dough into a ziplock bag and squeeze all air out of the bag. Refrigerate the dough overnight for mover flavor. Remove an hour before using it to bring it to room temperature.
