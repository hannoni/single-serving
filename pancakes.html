Pancakes

Makes 12

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, beat together vegetable oil, eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  2. In another bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. DO NOT mix the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients until the griddle is heated.
  4. Flip the pancakes.
