Hard Candy Recipe Lollipops

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup corn syrup
  • 3 drops food coloring
  • ½ teaspoon flavor oil = 10 droppers full

Directions

  1. Grease molds with cooking spray. Blot any excess spray with paper towels.
  2. Place greased molds onto a cooling rack than onto a greased jelly roll pan.
  3. Insert lollipop sticks.
  4. Combine the granulated sugar, water, and corn syrup in a pan and cook over medium heat (setting 5-6) stirring just until the sugar dissolves.
  5. Bring to a boil, add the thermometer, cook until the temperature reaches 250˚. Add food coloring and continue to cook until the temperature reaches 300˚.
  6. Remove from the stove at 295˚ as the temperature will continue rising. Do not leave the stove while the syrup is cooking.
  7. Let cool to 265˚. Add flavoring and cover for two minutes.
  8. Pour the hot candy into the molds being careful not to overflow them.
  9. After a tray is filled, roll the sticks to attach candy to them. (Be careful not to touch candy)
  10. Place the jelly roll pan on the window sill to cool.
  11. Remove the molds when the candy is cool and beginning to hold its shape. Continue cooling. Wrap in lollipop bags with a ribbon tied to each.
