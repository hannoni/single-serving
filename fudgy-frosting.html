Breakfast

Fudgy Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate pieces
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 ¼ powdered sugar

Directions

  1. In a large pan over low heat melt stir semisweet chocolate pieces and cup margarine.
  2. Cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in sour cream.
  4. Stir in sifted powdered sugar; stir until smooth.
  5. Frost the sides and top of a single layer cake.
